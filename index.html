Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before choosing your plan

Which plan should I choose?
For a well-structured preparation, the 3-month plan is the most popular choice. For outstanding value and time for multiple revision cycles, the 6-month plan is highly recommended. If your exam is just a few weeks away, the Ultimate Final Weeks Bundle gives you the most focused and exam-relevant preparation.

Yes — every core chapter access plan grants full access to all 30 high-yield, colour-coded, continuously updated chapters covering the complete PLAB 1 and UKMLA AKT curriculum, aligned with the Full UKMLA Content Map, Full PLAB 1 Blueprint, and the latest UK and NICE Guidelines. The only difference between plans is the access duration.
Why does the live platform matter — can I not just use older materials?
PLAB 1 and UKMLA AKT are constantly evolving. New recalls are integrated into the relevant chapters after every sitting, guidelines are updated, and question patterns shift. Static or older versions of any resource will inevitably be missing critical updates. The live Plab1Keys platform ensures you are always studying what is currently being tested. For some candidates, a single missing or outdated piece of information has been the difference between passing and needing a repeat attempt.
Are the Final Pre-Exam tools included in the core chapter plans?
No. The Revision Chapter, Big Mock, Extra Mock, and Ultimate Final Weeks Bundle are separate tools with their own 2-month access periods. They are designed to be added in the final weeks before your exam, alongside your core chapter preparation.
What does the Ultimate Final Weeks Bundle include?
The Ultimate Final Weeks Bundle includes the Revision Chapter, Big Mock, and Extra Mock — all with 2 months of access. All three are updated a few weeks before the real PLAB 1 sitting, incorporating the most recent real recalls, highest-probability reappearing concepts, and latest NICE updates.
What is Rev-Ref?
Rev-Ref stands for Revise and Refresh. It is a complementary resource containing 1,500+ rapid-recall scenarios designed to reinforce what you learn from the 30 Core Chapters and build stronger exam-day pattern recognition. It is not a replacement for the core chapters — it is a highly effective add-on that many candidates found significantly improved their retention and confidence.

Yes. Every plan is a one-time payment. There is no automatic renewal and no recurring charges of any kind. Your access runs for the selected duration and expires without any further charges unless you choose to purchase again.
